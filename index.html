First Date



Tip 1.

Be Yourself

They wouldn't be here if they didn't like you for you!

Tip 2.

Find Out What They Like

Once you find out the basics and some hobbies, you can have a conversation that could actually have some subtance, and who knows you might have something in common!

Tip 3.

Keep it Light

Throw out some jokes, let your sense of humor shine, and if the conversation leads to something a little deeper don't be afraid. Just be honest.

Tip 4.

Have Fun

If you had fun, and things didn't lead to a second date don't worry. You had a blast, and created new memories.

Second Date

Tip 1.

Be Confident

You're already here, so why not? They picked you and said yes again! You've got this!

Tip 2.

Try Something New

Maybe your partner told you about something you've never tried or done. If you are comfortable stepout and try it, and let them know. It's one more thing y'all can talk about.

Tip 3.

Don't Sweat The Small Stuff

They haven't texted you recently? They seem distant. Don't sweat it, you're a prize!

Tip 4.

Communicate

One of the key's to a succesful realtionship is communication. So don't be afriad to reachout and be vunerable. If they don't reach back out that's their loss, not yours.

6-Months

Recreate The First Date

Nostalgia

How fun would it be to go down memory land with your loved one. You can make it more romanctic this time. You both can laugh about how awkward you were or how much fun you had.

Make A Backyard Movie Night

Cozy

You can pick up all their favorite candies and drinks for the movie you are playing in the backyard. Rent a projector, lay down some pillows and blankets, maybe even by some lights to set the mood.

Plan a Scavenger Hunt

Adventurous/Thoughful

Show your thoughtful side by taking your partner on a scavenger hunt with some of your favorite memories together and some of their favortie memories as well!
